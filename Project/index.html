Background

The Peninsula carry mark system is to treat each student as an individual with specific needs. The system enables students to view their carrymark from time to time, this system also assist the lecturers in processing the mark and results of students.Therefore, students are allowed to exercise a certain degree of flexibility in order to optimize their performance, bearing in mind their own abilities, needs and interests.
